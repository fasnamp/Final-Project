# 😴 Sleep Health & Lifestyle Analysis

This project conducts an **in-depth analysis of sleep health and lifestyle patterns** using the *Sleep Health & Lifestyle dataset*. The goal is to explore how various factors such as **stress levels, physical activity, occupation, age, and sleep quality** are interrelated.

The insights derived aim to **support well-being, promote healthy habits, and inform preventive healthcare strategies.** 🩺

---

## 📊 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains **374 records** with key attributes including:

* 👤 Person ID
* 🚻 Gender
* 🎂 Age
* 💼 Occupation
* 🛌 Sleep Duration (hours)
* ⭐ Quality of Sleep (scale 1–10)
* 🏃 Physical Activity Level
* 😰 Stress Level (scale 1–10)
* ⚖️ BMI Category
* ❤️ Blood Pressure (Systolic and Diastolic)
* 👣 Daily Steps
* 💤 Sleep Disorder (if any)

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* 🔍 Analyze sleep health patterns across different **demographics and occupations**.
* 📉 Investigate the **impact of stress and physical activity on sleep quality**.
* 🩺 Study the relationship between **health indicators (blood pressure, BMI)** and sleep behavior.
* 🚨 Identify **at-risk groups** and recommend actionable **wellness strategies**.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used

* 🐍 **Python** – Data cleaning and exploratory analysis *(Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)*
* 🗄 **SQL** – Data querying and aggregation
* 📊 **Power BI & Tableau** – Interactive dashboards and visualizations
* 📑 **Excel** – Initial data analysis and dashboard creation

---

## 🔑 Key Insights

* 📉 Higher **stress levels** are strongly associated with **poor sleep quality**.
* 👩 Females tend to have **slightly longer sleep durations** than males.
* 💼 Occupational roles influence sleep patterns, with **high-stress jobs correlating with lower sleep quality**.
* ❤️ Blood pressure tends to **increase with age**, highlighting the importance of regular health monitoring.
* 🏃 Lifestyle factors such as **physical activity and sleep hygiene** significantly impact sleep health.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* 🧘 Implement **stress management and sleep hygiene programs**.
* 🏃 Promote **regular physical activity and healthy work-life balance**.
* 🩺 Conduct **routine health checks**, especially for blood pressure and BMI.
* 🎯 Design **targeted wellness initiatives** based on occupation and demographic trends.

---

## 📌 Conclusion

This analysis highlights the **critical influence of stress and lifestyle habits on sleep health**.

By utilizing **data-driven dashboards and analytics**, stakeholders can **monitor sleep patterns, identify health risks early, and implement effective intervention strategies** to enhance overall well-being. 🌙
